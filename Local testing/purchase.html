Frequently Asked Questions

Is Novelative a one-time purchase or a subscription?

Paying once for the Lifetime plan, or maintaining a monthly subscription, both give you indefinite support and updates to all of Novelative FOREVER.

Can I install Novelative on multiple computers?

Yes. The license allows you to install and use Novelative on up to three personal computers (e.g., desktop, laptop, tablet running the OS). The license key is tied to your account for easy management.

Is there a free trial?

You can try Novelative for free. The first 30 days of use are completely free. After the first 30 days of use, you can pay $50 one time for unlimited use, or $5/month for unlimited use while you hold the subscription.
