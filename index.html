Tagging System

Powerful Organization, Personalized for You

Novelative's tagging system allows you to organize your files YOUR way, integrated systems allthroughout the app make it a breeze to visualize your project in a variety of useful ways.

  • Custom Tags: Customize the appearance of every tag and apply as many tags as needed for every file.
  • Tagging Utility: Tags appear in the hierarchy next to your file's name. But tags are also visualized inside canvases. And use the Graph View to visualize your tagged files in a whole new way.
Graph View

Visualize & Navigate.

Use Novelative's Graph View to navigate and organize your files to get a view of your project.

  • Tag View: Every file is organized into groups of tags, easy for navigating files with certain tags.
  • Link View: Show's the links between all your files that have hyperlinks.

The "Accidental" App

I never planned to create an app for the public, or to ever launch anything. I was deeply into world-building, and used a professional desktop app to create and store my world. But organizing all my thoughts and ideas was a mess.

So I decided I'd make my own app—just for myself. And it worked!

It wasn't until I realized that my self-made app had solved a REAL problem. It's impossible to navigate your complex worlds with standard tools. Novelative is the answer to that problem.
