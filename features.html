Projects

Manage multiple distinct story universes entirely offline with dedicated save files and local data privacy.

Wiki-Linking

Connect your universe effortlessly. Reference characters, settings, and lore directly within your manuscript.

Word Comment Links

Leave notes for your future self. Highlight specific words or phrases to anchor contextual margin notes directly tied to your text.

Intelligent Tagging

Organize by concept, not just by folder. Apply global tags to chapters, characters, and notes to quickly filter your entire project workspace.

Infinite Canvas

Plot visually on an unlimited whiteboard. Snap cards together, draw orthogonal routing lines, and structure complex timelines.

Dynamic Graph View

Visualize your story's architecture by switching between Tags view to see thematic clusters and Links view to trace connections.

Custom Themes

Personalize the entire application interface with pre-set light and dark modes or design your own custom color palettes.

Editor Notes

Insert distinct, non-printing text blocks directly into your manuscript flow for scene goals, reminders, or directorial notes.

Paged vs Continuous

Toggle your editor view between a traditional print-preview layout with page breaks and an infinite digital scroll.

Split View

Open two documents side-by-side to reference your outline or character sheets while drafting your chapters.

Focus vs Markup

Switch between a distraction-free clean writing interface and a detailed editing mode that reveals comments and meta-data.

Synonym Finder

Access a built-in thesaurus instantly to find the perfect word without leaving your writing environment.

Custom Dictionary

Teach the spellchecker your unique world-building terms and character names to stop red underlines.

Find & Replace

Search for and update text across a single document or your entire project library at once.

Styled Sections

Create and apply reusable formatting presets for recurring elements like chapter headers, blockquotes, or scene breaks.

Backups

Automatically save rolling snapshots of your work locally to prevent data loss without needing a cloud subscription.

Typewriter Mode

Keep your active typing line locked to the vertical center of the screen to reduce eye strain and maintain focus.
